Nowhere Near You

Nowhere Near You is an exploration of theatrical design through the creation of a fake play, "Blood Runs Cold," a horror comedy musical from 2022. The website acts as an archive of materials and designs realated to this show, creating the illusion that "Blood Runs Cold." is a real play.

Everything on the website (and the website itself) is handmade: the playbill, costume designs, set designs, reviews, and articles surrounding the performance. The project is both a foray into theatrical design, and an exploration of the ephemeral neature of theater - what gets left behind when a show closes?
